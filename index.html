RxJS challenge:

Before starting the challenge, click on the Fork button above to copy this project to your workspace.

Open the console below to see results.

The challenge is to use RxJS operators to manipulate the output of the source stream, following the rules below:

  • Discard any number ending with 0 or 5.
  • For odd numbers, print them to the console in groups of 3. Example: [3, 27, 61]
  • For even numbers, repeat each emittion 2 times.
  • The maximum number of elements printed to the console is 30, then the emittions must stop, and the word 'complete' must be printed 1x only. (element = A group of odd numbers || an even number)

Example of output:

26
26
[1, 17, 21]
4
4

.
[19, 99, 67]
complete
